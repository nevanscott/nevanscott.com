Nevan Scott

Hi there! I run Button School from my home base in Barcelona. My time is split between teaching, developing new courses, and growing the Button School community.

I’ve been teaching for a while now (15 years?), and I absolutely love working with students. My background is in both design and development, primarily for web stuff and design systems.

These days, you can find me teaching coding for designers and interface and interaction design. If either of these interest you, I’d love to have you in class!

I occasionally write at notated.org.

Drop me a line at nevan@buttonschool.com
